Legal

If you are familiar with my work, you probably know that your privacy is important to me. This is why I have designed this website to collect minimal data. I only collect data for analytics, and advertisements to support my livelihood.

I try to keep advertisements at a minimum and use less disruptive alternatives. If you want to support this goal, consider giving me a donation.

If you are concerned about data collection from such services, I strongly recommend using a privacy-friendly browser like LibreWolf alongside uBlock Origin.

Privacy Policy

Analytics

I use Google Analytics to track general website traffic, such as page views and visitor locations. IP addresses are anonymized and no personally identifiable information (PII), such as names or emails, is collected.

Cookies

This site uses cookies only to support analytics. No personal data is stored in these cookies.

Advertising

I may use Google AdSense for advertising. These services use cookies and similar technologies to display ads tailored to your interests. Also, they may collect anonymous information about your visits to this and other websites to provide personalized advertising.

Data Sharing

Collected data is not sold or shared with third parties, except as required by the analytics or advertising providers for reporting purposes.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using your browser settings or tools like Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can also opt out of personalized ads from Google via Ads Settings

Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. Please check back for any changes.
